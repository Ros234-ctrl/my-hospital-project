About Us

Welcome to Neurology Hospital, a leading institution in neurology care. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive treatment and care for patients with neurological disorders.

Our team of experienced neurologists and medical staff are committed to delivering the highest standard of care using state-of-the-art technology and innovative treatment approaches.

Our Services

  • Diagnosis and treatment of neurological conditions
  • Neurological surgery
  • Neurorehabilitation
  • Electroencephalography (EEG)
  • Neuroimaging (MRI, CT scan, etc.)
  • Neuropsychological testing
